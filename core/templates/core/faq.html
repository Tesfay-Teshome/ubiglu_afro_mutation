General Questions

Ubiglu Afro Mutation is a digital asset management and project collaboration platform that helps creators showcase and monetize their work while connecting with others in the community.

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, complete your profile, and start uploading your projects. Our platform guides you through each step of the process.

Account & Profile

You can change your password by going to your profile settings. Click on your profile picture, select "Settings," and choose "Change Password."

We recommend maintaining only one account per user. This helps maintain transparency and trust within our community.

Projects & Content

You can share various types of digital assets including designs, artwork, templates, and creative projects. All content must comply with our content guidelines.

We take intellectual property rights seriously. You can watermark your previews, set usage terms, and choose appropriate licenses for your work.

Payments & Transactions

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and various digital payment methods through our secure payment processor, Stripe.

Refund requests are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our support team within 14 days of purchase if you experience any issues.
